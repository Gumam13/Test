Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I go live with Strukly?
You can be up and running in minutes. Just sign up, set up your outlets and products, and start using Strukly immediately.
Do I need any special hardware?
No. Strukly works on any browser and most devices (laptop, tablet, POS terminal). For receipt printing, just connect any standard receipt printer.
Can I use my own QRIS for payments?
Yes! Strukly is compatible with QRIS and supports instant validation for seamless customer payment experiences.
What happens after the free trial?
You can choose a plan and continue using all your data and settings—no disruption. If you don’t upgrade, your data is safe and can be exported anytime.
Is there a support team if I need help?
Absolutely. Our helpdesk is ready to support you by email or WhatsApp, including onboarding and troubleshooting.
